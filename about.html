About Muskan Sharma

Hi, I’m Muskan Sharma, a passionate tarot reader with one year of experience, continuously learning and evolving in this mystical journey. Through my YouTube channel, I have connected with many people, offering intuitive guidance and deep insights.

Tarot is more than just card reading—it’s about creating a safe, judgment-free space where you can share your thoughts, fears, and dreams. I treat every client like family and offer guidance with honesty, warmth, and compassion. My mission is to make tarot accessible to everyone at an affordable price.
